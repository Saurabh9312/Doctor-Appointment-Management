2. ABOUT THE HOSPITAL

Sunrise Multispeciality Hospital is a 450-bed, NABH-accredited, tertiary care hospital offering advanced healthcare with modern equipment, senior specialists, and 24×7 emergency support.
The hospital provides integrated medical, surgical, and diagnostic services with a focus on patient safety, transparent billing, and evidence-based treatment.
